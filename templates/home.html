What is LyriGist AI?

LyriGist AI is an innovative artificial intelligence designed to analyze English song lyrics and accurately determine their genre. By examining linguistic patterns, themes, and stylistic elements, this AI simplifies the process of genre identification, offering valuable insights into the diverse landscape of English-language music. Whether you're a music enthusiast, researcher, or industry professional, LyriGist AI helps you explore and understand English music more efficiently
